Lemon Pudding Cake

Prep Time:12min

Price:R55.99

Yield: 5

Serving Size:10

Ingridients








steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8-inch square baking dish.
  2. Beat egg yolks, lemon juice, butter, and lemon zest together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until thick and lemon colored.
  3. an overhead shot of eggs, lemon juice, butter, and lemon zest mixed together
  4. Combine sugar, flour, and salt; add alternately with milk to the lemon mixture, beating well after each addition.
  5. an overhead mixture of sugar, flour, salt, and milk beaten with lemon mixture in a stand mixer
  6. Beat egg whites in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl until stiff.
  7. an overhead shot of egg whites beaten until stiff in a bowl
  8. Blend egg whites into batter on low speed with an electric mixer. Pour into prepared baking dish.
  9. an overhead shot of lemon pudding batter in a baking dish
  10. Place a pan of hot water in the preheated oven; set the baking dish into the pan; bake for 45 minutes. For best results, serve warm.
  11. close up on a single slice of lemon pudding cake topped with confectioners' sugar
