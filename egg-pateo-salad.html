Egg Salad Pateo

Prep Time:8min

Price:R68.99

Yield: 10

Serving Size:5

Ingridients













steps

  1. Place the eggs in a medium sized saucepan along with some water.
  2. Bring the water to boiling point.
  3. Once the water has boiled, cover the saucepan with a lid and leave for the desired amount of time
  4. Once the desired time has come to pass, place the eggs in cold water (this will stop the eggs from cooking any further) for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Discard the cold water, peel the eggs and use accordingly.
  6. To make the salad
  7. Arrange the salad bed by first laying down the rocket, followed by the tomatoes, cucumber and the eggs.
  8. Finally garnish with carrot and pink salt, garlic pepper and chives.
  9. Whisk together the ingredients for the dressing in a small bowl and drizzle over the salad
