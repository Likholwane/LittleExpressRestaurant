Red Velvet Brownies

Prep Time:15min

Price:R68.99

Yield: 16

Serving Size:16

Ingridients















  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/355°F. Grease and line an 8-inch square pan with baking/parchment paper. Let the baking paper hang over the sides to easily remove the brownie once baked and cooled.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, melt the butter and chocolate in the microwave or over low heat on the stove, or by using a bain-mairie method. Whisk the melted butter and chocolate together until smooth and shiny.
  3. Add the white and brown sugar to the chocolate-butter mixture and beat in until mostly dissolved. This may require a bit of additional heating in the microwave or over low heat.
  4. serve 5min after baking.
