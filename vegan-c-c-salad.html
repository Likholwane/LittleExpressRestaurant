Vegan Chicken Ceeaser Salad

Prep Time:6min

Price:R69.99

Yield: 6

Serving Size:3

Ingridients











steps

  1. Cut or tear bread into 1-2 inch pieces and place on a baking tray. Drizzle with olive oil and place in the oven for 4-5 minutes, turning half way until golden on both sides. Remove from oven and set aside.
  2. Fry vegan chicken pieces in a tbsp of oil for 5-7 minutes or according to packet instructions. Set aside.
  3. Add all the dressing ingredients into a blender or food processor. Blitz until smooth. Add a tbsp of water to thin if needed.
  4. Rinse romaine lettuce leaves and dry. Cut or tear into large pieces and place in a large bowl.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and dressing and toss well. Add the croutons before serving and finish with some shavings of parmesan.
